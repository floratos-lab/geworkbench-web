What's New

geWorkbench (genomics Workbench) is an open source application that provides access to an integrated suite of tools for the analysis and visualization of data from a wide range of genomics domains (gene expression, sequence, protein structure and systems biology). The full desktop version of geWorkbench has more than 70 distinct plug-in modules implementing both classical analyses (several variants of clustering, classification, homology detection, etc.) as well as state of the art algorithms for the reverse engineering of regulatory networks and for protein structure prediction, among many others.

This site (geWorkbench-Web) provides a web interface to a subset of plug-ins. Over time, we expect that all geWorkbench modules will be available through the web interface.

geWorkbench and geWorkbench-Web are funded through MAGNet, the National Center for the Multiscale Analysis of Genomic and Cellular Networks. MAGNet is a member of the National Centers for Biomedical Computing (NCBCs).
